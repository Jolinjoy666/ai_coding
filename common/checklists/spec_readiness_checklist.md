# Spec Readiness Checklist

- **Goal clear**: Design purpose and scope are clear.
- **Functional requirements clear**: Required behavior is measurable.
- **Interfaces clear**: Ports, protocols, clock/reset, and sideband behavior are identified.
- **PPA targets clear**: Latency, throughput, frequency, area, and power goals are known or marked unknown.
- **Memory behavior clear**: Capacity, access pattern, latency, collision, and reset/init behavior are identified.
- **Error behavior clear**: Illegal inputs, overflow, timeout, and recovery are specified or questioned.
- **Assumptions separated**: Facts, assumptions, and open questions are distinct.
- **Verification intent seeded**: Basic directed, random, assertion, and coverage needs are visible.
