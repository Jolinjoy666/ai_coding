# Regression Closure Checklist

- **Pass criteria defined**: Required tests, seeds, and coverage targets are known.
- **Failures triaged**: All failures are classified and assigned disposition.
- **Reproduction preserved**: Failing commands, seeds, logs, and waves are saved.
- **Known issues tracked**: Waived or deferred failures have rationale.
- **Coverage reviewed**: Functional and code coverage gaps are understood.
- **Recent fixes rerun**: Changed areas have targeted reruns.
- **No infrastructure noise**: Environment, license, and flow failures are separated from design failures.
- **Summary generated**: Regression status is readable by test and seed.
